1. Privacy Policy Last Updated: January 26, 2026 1. Introduction Cricket Manager 25 ("the Game") is a non-commercial, browser-based simulation game. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a deep tactical cricket management experience. This policy explains how we handle your data. 2. Data We Collect Authentication Data: When you log in via Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information through Supabase Auth to create and manage your game account. Game Saves: Your game progress is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data stays on your machine and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly export it. Support & Feedback: When you submit bug reports or feedback, we collect your description, optional email, device information (OS, browser), and console logs to help us improve the game. Infrastructure Data: We use Cloudflare tools for domain management and security, which may process standard log data (like IP addresses) to protect against attacks. 3. How We Use Your Data To provide and maintain your game account. To identify and fix technical issues reported through our bug reporting system. To communicate with you regarding support requests if an email was provided. 4. Data Retention & Third Parties Retention: Bug reports and feedback data are retained for at least one year to assist in long-term development and trend analysis. Third Parties: We share data only with necessary service providers: Google (Authentication), Supabase (Database/Auth), Cloudflare (Infrastructure), and Web3Forms (Form processing). We do not sell or trade your personal information. 5. Age Requirement Users must be at least 13 years old to play Cricket Manager 25. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. 6.
